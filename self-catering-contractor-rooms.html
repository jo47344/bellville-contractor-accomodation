How Contractors Actually Use Self-Catering (Real Strategies)

Based on conversations with contractors who've stayed at self-catering accommodation for extended projects, here's what actually works:

Sunday Batch Cooking

Spend 2-3 hours on Sunday afternoon cooking for the week. Make a big pot of stew, grill 10 chicken breasts, cook a pasta bake, roast vegetables. Store everything in containers. Now you have 5 days of dinners ready to reheat in 5 minutes when you get back from a 10-hour workday.

Breakfast Routine

Buy a loaf of bread, butter, jam, eggs, and coffee on Sunday. Every morning: toast + eggs + coffee = R15 instead of R80 at a cafe. Over 30 days, this saves R1,950 just on breakfast.

Packed Lunches

Make sandwiches the night before (bread, cheese, cold meat, tomato). Pack an apple or banana. Total cost: R25 vs R100 at a garage or takeout. Saves R2,250/month.

Smart Shopping

Checkers, Pick n Pay, and Shoprite are all within 3km of Bellville South. Buy in bulk on Sunday (chicken, mince, rice, pasta, canned tomatoes, frozen vegetables). These basics last all week and cost a fraction of daily takeout.
